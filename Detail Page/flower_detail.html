About

Beautiful in form, lilies are one of the most popular and versatile flowers in the world. Coming in a variety of colors and known for its strong fragrance, this elegant bloom is a show-stopper on its own while also serving as the perfect compliment to any bouquet. In fact, lilies are one of the most popular flowers in the world.

There are over 100 different types of true lilies belonging to the Lilium genus. Lilies are found predominantly in the northern hemisphere.

Symbolism

Lilies have different meanings throughout different cultures, but generally, these amazing blooms represent grace, royalty, beauty, and rebirth. As with other flowers, meaning change based on color so when choosing lilies to gift someone, keep the below guidelines in mind.

Lilies are the birth flower for May babies. They are also the traditional flower for 30th wedding anniversaries. White lilies are one of the most popular funeral flowers as, by representing purity and giving off a fragrant and distinct scent, they provide comfort to the bereaved.

Care Tips

  • When the flowers bloom, they are large, colorful, and often fragrant.
  • True Lilies all have 6 petals and 6 stamens with anthers (oblong pods that contain pollen).
  • Water regularly, especially during active growth.
  • Lilies are poisonous to cats, so keep your furry friends away from them.
  • Remove faded flowers to encourage more blooms and prevent the plant from wasting energy on seed production.
